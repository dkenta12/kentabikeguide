San Francisco + Peninsula

Bike San Francisco.

Hi, I run Kenta Bike Guide myself and I help people plan and ride routes around San Francisco and the Peninsula. Some folks want an easy shorter spin, others want to go the full 25 miles. I will help you pick a route that matches your pace and make sure you feel prepared before we roll out. My goal is to make you more comfortorable with exploring the bay on bike!

  • Ride distance: 0 to 25 miles
  • Region: San Francisco and Peninsula
  • Pace: beginner-friendly to steady endurance
  • Format: planning call, guided rides, and small groups

Guided Tour Levels

Choose the level of support that best matches your goals. Every option starts with a route planning Zoom call tailored to your ride and comfort level.

Level Details
Level 1: Route Plan Session We meet on Zoom to discuss what kind of ride you want. I suggest a route and send you a map so you can ride confidently on your own.
$40 per planning session
Level 2: Guided Ride (1 Rider) Includes the full planning call plus I join you for the ride. I bring spare tubes, a pump, and patch kits. Please bring your own bike and helmet, or I can help you rent one. A 25-mile ride at a relaxed pace is usually around 3 hours.
$195 per guided ride
Level 3: Group Guided Ride (Up to 3) Same support as Level 2, built for larger groups up to 3 riders. We can add coffee stops and lunch spots to make the day more enjoyable and social.
$360 per group ride

Meeting Up & Transportation

Flexible meetups are available based on your location, route type, and group size.

  • Start from your location: I can bike or drive to your home to begin the ride when needed and practical.
  • Ride start shuttle: I can drive myself, one passenger, and up to two bikes to a selected starting point.
  • Plan ahead: we will confirm pickup and start logistics before ride day so everything runs smoothly.

Gear & Preparation

Bringing your own setup is preferred, but alternatives are available.

  • Please bring your own bike, helmet, water, and food.
  • If that is not possible, we can discuss alternate options in advance.
  • Bike availability can be arranged for an additional fee.

Route Style

Routes are matched to your skill level and comfort on the bike.

  • Most rides focus on road cycling in San Francisco and the Peninsula.
  • Gravel segments can be added by request.
  • Route planning prioritizes clear navigation, safety, and enjoyable pacing.

Important Notes & Safety

  • This guide service does not include insurance. Riders who require insurance should arrange their own coverage.
  • Safety is always the top priority during planning and on-ride guidance.
  • If conditions become unsafe due to sudden weather changes or other risks, the tour may be immediately canceled.
